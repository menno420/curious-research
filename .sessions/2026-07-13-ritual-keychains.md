## 💡 Session idea

**One parametric-text engine → a family of labeled workshop parts.** The generator that stamps
a name onto a keychain base is the same engine that stamps a label onto anything flat: feed it a
different base profile — a cable clip, a spool-end tag, a drawer face — and the identical "your
text, your color, batch a plateful" pipeline turns out labeled shop organizers, not just gifts.
It links straight to [`drawer-organizer-generator.md`](../ideas/drawer-organizer-generator.md):
the keychain factory and the organizer generator are the same parametric-text machine wearing two
base shapes, so building one meaningfully de-risks the other.
