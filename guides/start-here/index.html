Welcome — here's your new workshop companion

This repo was built for you: a place where your printers, your robot arm, and your Arduino bench get a research partner that teaches by showing. Here's a first lap through it — about 30 minutes, all in your browser. Press Play all, or step through with Next.

What you just watched

Your first 30 minutes, four stops: open a guide → snap a photo and ask Claude → peek at what's possible → watch a change land safely. Do them in order, or jump around — none of it is homework.

What's in your workshop companion

  • guides/ — your growing visual textbook: animated explainers you watch in a browser, each with a read-it-instead companion. This tour lives here.
  • ideas/ — one file per idea, matched to your gear. A menu, never a to-do list.
  • research/ — honest deep-dives on what your bench can really do with Claude, with the limits marked instead of hidden.
  • The PR loop — the one safe way every change lands. Nothing merges that could break anything, so you can experiment freely.

Honest notes: the minute estimates are a rough shape, not a stopwatch — go as slow as you like, and an empty week is completely fine. Safety is real, too: Claude designs, but you slice every print, power the servos from their own supply, and watch the arm move (see CLAUDE.md §2).
